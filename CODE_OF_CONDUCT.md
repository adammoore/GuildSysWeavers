# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

The Guild of Systems Weavers is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, neurodivergence, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, nationality, or career path.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and considerate in communication
- Welcome diverse perspectives and experience
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and its knowledge commons
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Respect that practitioners come from many different sectors, career paths, and backgrounds

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Dismissive or demeaning language about career paths, roles, or expertise
- Personal attacks or ad hominem arguments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Gatekeeping — making people feel unwelcome based on their background or experience level
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all guild spaces, including the GitHub repository, issues, pull requests, and any other community channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the guild maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter.
