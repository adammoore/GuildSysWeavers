# Guild of Systems Weavers

The **Guild of Systems Weavers** is a public professional commons for a neglected but vital kind of cross-boundary infrastructure practitioner.

> Systems Weavers are practitioners who connect systems, teams, data, workflows and institutional realities across boundaries where formal ownership is fragmented or absent.

This repository is the seed of a guild intended to recognise, develop, and advocate for the craft of cross-system infrastructure work: the skilled labour of joining technologies, teams, workflows, data, and institutional realities into functioning services.

## Why this exists

Many infrastructure and transformation projects depend on people who can work across technical, organisational, and procedural boundaries. These practitioners are often described informally as "glue people", "plumbers", "Swiss army knives", or "jacks of all trades", but those labels fail to dignify the sophistication of the work.

This project exists to support:

- **Recognition**: giving this work language and legitimacy
- **Codification**: turning tacit practice into shared patterns and methods
- **Professionalisation**: building portable, contribution-led standing outside narrow organisational ladders

## Repository structure

- `docs/` — public site content for GitHub Pages
- `docs/roles/` — role glossary and adjacent terms
- `docs/patterns/` — starter pattern library
- `docs/case-studies/` — case study materials
- `docs/community/` — community participation materials
- `docs/governance/` — governance and decision-making notes
- `templates/` — reusable markdown templates
- `.github/` — issue and PR templates
